About Me

I'm Evan

PhD Researcher Fellow

I have received the B.Eng. degree in mechanical engineering from Hainan University, China, and the joint M.Sc. degree in sensor and imaging system from The University of Glasgow, Glasgow, U.K., and The University of Edinburgh, Edinburgh, U.K. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in The University of Osaka, Japan. My previous research interests include path planning and tracking control of magnetic microrobots.
